Sea battle rules

In Sea Battle game you play against an opponent, both of you have two 10x10 grids, one grid is for your own ships, and another one is for your opponent's ships which you are trying to discover. You start by arranging your ships on your grid. You have these kinds of ships:

  1. Aviation Carrier (1 ship)
  2. Battleship(2 ships)
  3. Cruiser (3 ships)
  4. Destroyer (4 ships)

The ships cannot be next to each other on the grid, not even diagonally, they must always be at least one square away from one another. Also every ship except destroyer consists of multiple parts which you cannot mix together with parts of different ships and to see the difference between them every part has corresponding size label. You can arrange them as you want by dragging and dropping them following ship placement rules.

After the game started players take turns clicking(shooting) on the grid. If you miss the turn changes and if you hit a ship you get to shoot again. You mark which squares are hits and which are misses and try to be the first to sink all your opponent's ships. The first player to sink their opponent's ships or the first player whose enemy loses all his time wins the game.

Magic video camera üìπ feature: When you destroyed a ship for which destruction there should be an award in a kind of magic video camera, then for 10 seconds hovering effect on ships will be different from hovering effect on empty cells.
